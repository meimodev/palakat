# Palakat - Church Management Platform

Palakat is a church management system designed for Indonesian churches (HKBP denomination). The platform helps manage:

- Church membership and organizational structure (columns, positions)
- Activities and events with approval workflows
- Financial tracking (revenues, expenses) with account numbers
- Song books (NKB, NNBT, KJ, DSL hymnals)
- Documents and reports
- Church registration requests

## Target Users

- Church administrators (web admin panel)
- Church members (mobile app)
- Church leadership for approvals

## Key Domain Concepts

- **Church**: Organization with members, columns (groups), and positions
- **Membership**: Church member with baptism/sidi status, linked to account
- **Activity**: Events/services requiring supervisor and approver workflow
- **Bipra**: Church organizational divisions (PKB, WKI, PMD, RMJ, ASM)
- **Approval Rules**: Configurable rules linking activity types, financial types, and positions
- **Financial Account Numbers**: Chart of accounts for revenue/expense categorization
