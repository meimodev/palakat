# Design System

This document outlines the core visual style and components of our design system.

## Brand Identity

Our brand identity is built on a foundation of modern aesthetics and clear communication.

### Color Palette

We utilize a rich magenta-led palette to strengthen brand recognition while keeping the neutral surface system calm and readable.

* **Primary Color:** `#921573` (Magenta) - Used for primary actions, headlines, and key elements.
* **Secondary Color:** `#6B1D84` (Violet) - Supports primary elements and less prominent UI components.
* **Tertiary Color:** `#B81D5B` (Rose) - An accent for highlights, dividers, or subtle emphasis.
* **Neutral Color:** `#F8F8F8` (Off-White) - The foundational background color, providing visual breathing room.

### Typography

We employ a distinct typographic hierarchy to enhance readability and brand personality.

* **Headlines:** `manrope` - Strong and modern for titles and headings.
* **Body Text:** `inter` - Highly legible and versatile for all body copy.
* **Labels:** `inter` - Clear and concise for form labels and small text elements.

### Shape & Form

Our design language features a balanced approach to UI element shapes.

* **Roundedness:** `2` (Moderate) - Providing a friendly yet professional feel with slightly rounded corners.

### Spacing

We maintain a consistent and comfortable level of whitespace throughout the interface.

* **Spacing:** `2` (Normal) - Ensuring elements are well-separated without feeling sparse or cluttered.
